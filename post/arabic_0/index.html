Learn Arabic from scratch (0)

August 01, 2024
Language

[Lesson 1-5] Embark on an exciting adventure into the beauty of the Arabic language with comprehensive courses designed especially for absolute beginners. Thank Asmae for her elaborate YouTube channel - “Learn Arabic with Asmae” (https://www.youtube.com/@learnarabicwithasmae1).

Lesson 1

No. Arabic English
1 (ana) [laa] ureedu dhaalik I [don’t] want this
2 (ana) ureedu an ataa’llam Al-a’rabiyya I want to learn Arabic

Lesson 2

No. Arabic English
1 (ana) [laa] ureedu an afhamu Al-a’rabiyya qaleelan I [don’t] want to understand Arabic a little bit
2 (ana) afhamu Al-a’rabiyya jayyidan I understand Arabic very well

Lesson 3

No. Arabic English
1 (ana) atakallamu Al-Injleeziyya wal-a’rabiyya I speak English and Arabic
2 (ana) afhamu Al-Injleeziyya wa ureedu an ataa’llam al-a’rabiya I understand English and I want to learn Arabic
3 (ana) ureedu an atakallamu Al-a’rabiyya maa’ka I want to speak Arabic with you
4 Naa’m, ana ayyidan Yes, me too
5 (ana) ayyidan ureedu an ataa’llamu Al-a’rabiyya I want to learn Arabic too
6 (ana) ayyidan atakallamu Al-Injleeziyya jayyidan I speak English very well too

Lesson 4

No. Arabic English
1 (ana) [lastu] min huna I’m [not] from here
2 (ana) [laa] a’eeshu huna I [don’t] live here
3 (ana) a’eeshu huna munthu sana I’ve been living here for a year
4 (ana) a’eeshu huna munthu zaman Taweel I’ve been living here for a long time
5 (ana) huna munthu zaman Taweel I’ve been here for a long time
6 (ana) a’eeshu fee Dubei I live in Dubai

Lesson 5

No. Arabic English
1 Yumkinuni an ataa’llam bisuraa' I can learn fast
2 (Hal) Yumkinuni an ataa’llam Al-a’rabiyya bisuraa’ ? Can I learn Arabic quickly?
3 (Hal) Yumkinuni an adhhaba al-a’n I can go now
4 Laa Yumkinuni an adhhaba al-an I can’t go now
5 ahssant Good job
6 Laa Yumkinuni an a’eesha ba’da al-an I can’t live here anymore
