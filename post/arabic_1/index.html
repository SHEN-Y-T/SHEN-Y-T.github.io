No. Arabic English
1 (ana) ladaeeya a’mal muhim laahiqan I have important work later
2 (ana) ladaeeya a’mal al-an I have work now
3 laa yumkinuni an adhhaba al-an I can’t go now
4 ana a’malu huna I work here
5 laa yumkinuni an a’malu huna ba’da al-an I can’t work here anymore
6 [laisa] ladaeeya a’mal al-an I [don’t] have work now
